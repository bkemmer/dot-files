1. Disable Menu Bar Transparency
Open System Settings
Go to Accessibility
Click Display
Find Reduce transparency
Turn it OFF

2. Changes between ESC, Caps Lock, Control and Fn
Open System Settings
Go to Keyboard
Click Modifier Keys
Change Caps Lock to ESC 
Change Control to Fn
Change Fn to Control
Also change F1 to F12 to show always

3. Autohide Dock
Open System Settings
Go to Desktop & Dock
Find Automatically hide and show the Dock
Turn it ON
